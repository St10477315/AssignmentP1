Location & Travel Times

  • Stay Easy Pretoria to Clarens: 3 hr 51 min (347.8 km) via N3 and R26
  • Clarens to Qhanolla Guesthouse: 45 min (52 km) via R711
  • Qhanolla Guesthouse to AfriSki: 1 hr 19 min (75 km)

Snack & Refreshments

  • Please pack enough snacks and drinks for the road and the weekend, as there are no nearby shops once we reach our destination.

Tourism Levy

  • Effective 1 April 2024, Lesotho's border authorities introduced a R100.00 tourism levy per person for foreign passport holders. This must be paid at the border gate.

Accomodation

  • We will be staying at Qhanolla Guesthouse in Botha-Bothe.
  • Please bring your own charging plugs for mobile phones and other devices.
